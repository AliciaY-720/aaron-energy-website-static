We provide residential and commercial natural gas piping services, including new gas line installations, extensions, appliance connections, and backup generator gas hookups. All work is performed by an Enbridge-authorized contractor and completed in accordance with applicable codes, safety requirements, and inspection standards.

Our scope includes meter-area piping, shut-off valve installation, pressure-rated fittings, and system troubleshooting. We focus on safe, clean, and code-compliant installations that integrate reliably with existing gas infrastructure.
